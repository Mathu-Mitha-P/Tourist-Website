Nestled at the base of the sacred Arunachala Hill, this town is renowned for the Annamalaiyar Temple, a magnificent Dravidian-style temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. With its towering gopurams, intricate carvings, and serene ambiance, the temple attracts devotees and architecture enthusiasts from around the world. One of the highlights of Thiruvannamalai is the Girivalam or circumambulation of Arunachala Hill, a spiritual journey undertaken by devotees during full moon nights. The 14-kilometer path is dotted with ancient shrines and offers a tranquil, meditative experience. The Ramana Ashram, the abode of the revered sage Ramana Maharshi, is another must-visit for those seeking peace and self-discovery. For nature lovers, the town's lush surroundings and the majestic Arunachala Hill provide a picturesque backdrop. Adventure enthusiasts can enjoy trekking trails that lead to scenic viewpoints. Traditional South Indian cuisine, including the local delicacies, adds to the experience. Whether you seek spirituality, cultural exploration, or natural beauty, Thiruvannamalai promises a profound and unforgettable journey.
